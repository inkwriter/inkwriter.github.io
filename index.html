Background

About me

I'm an IT support specialist covering 17 convenience stores plus a handful of other sites across eastern Kentucky and southwestern Virginia. The work keeps me moving — roughly a third of my week is windshield time — across endpoints, networking, payment hardware, and the kind of problems that don't have a knowledge base article yet.

When something slows me down more than once, I build a tool for it. That's led to Python reporting scripts, LAN utilities, web-based maintenance apps, and a ticketing dashboard my team actually uses.

On the side I write D&D modules, volunteer on missing persons OSINT investigations, and study toward a cybersecurity path — currently working through the Google Cybersecurity Certificate and Network+ material, with hands-on time in Kali.
